# 🌙 Jordanian RamaDiet 🇯🇴

**RamaDiet** is an AI-powered, interactive Ramadan meal planner designed to generate customized Suhoor and Iftar plans using authentic Jordanian cuisine. 

Built with Streamlit and the Google Gemini API, this tool dynamically adjusts food portions based on your specific fitness goals (like building muscle or losing fat), daily activity levels, and medical conditions. 

## ✨ Key Features
* **🤖 AI-Powered Customization:** Uses Gemini 2.5 Flash to analyze your stats and generate the perfect macro-balanced meal plan.
* **🥘 Authentic Jordanian Meals:** Features local favorites like Mansaf, Maqluba, and Mujadara, intelligently portioned for your goals.
* **🔄 Smart Swaps:** Craving Qatayef? The AI provides healthy, localized alternatives so you stay on track without feeling deprived.
* **🎛️ Interactive Macros:** Adjust ingredient weights on the fly and watch your total calories, protein, and carbs update instantly.
* **🏥 Medical Context Aware:** Safely filters high-sodium or high-sugar ingredients for users with conditions like hypertension or diabetes.
* **🛒 Auto-Grocery List:** Generates a downloadable shopping list based on your custom plan.
* **💧 Hydration Tracker:** Calculates exact water intake needs between Iftar and Suhoor.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend/UI:** Streamlit
* **Data Handling:** Pandas
* **AI/LLM:** Google Generative AI (Gemini 2.5 Flash)
* **Language:** Python 3.x
